Business

Description

Sector: Health Care
Industry: Medical Instruments & Equipment

Baxter International Inc. is an American health care company headquartered in Deerfield, Illinois. The company primarily focuses on products to treat hemophilia, kidney disease, immune disorders and other chronic and acute medical conditions.

Industry

They provide a broad portfolio of essential healthcare products including:

Sterile IV solutions

Inhaled anesthetics

Acute and chronic dialysis therapies





While Baxter does not sell something new or innovative, they do have a slight advantage by participating in emerging markets.

Their products are manufactured in nearly 20 countries and are currently sold in over 100 countries.

In 2017, the company generated approximately 60% of its revenues outside of the United States.





Outside Influences

Positives

Normally this would not be a positive and it is a growing problem that needs to be solved

If you are overweight or obese, you have a greater chance of developing diabetes or high blood pressure, which are the leading causes of kidney disease and kidney failure.

In fact, 20-40% of diabetes patients develop renal disease. And if their condition is left untreated, they will require dialysis several times a week.

The number of dialysis patients in these regions is also expected to grow at a faster pace than in developed countries.

Negatives

Increasingly competetive and regulated environment

Fluctuating foreign currency exchange rates

Role in Portfolio and Recommendation

Baxter makes up just under 2% of our portfolio

It has a beta of 0.89

It's dividend yield is just under 1%, but has been steadily growing

Both Stryker and Medtronic are in the medical devices industry and have global presences

Baxter's products vary from either of theirs and since Baxter is mainly in emerging markets, it has solid footing and still provides diversity in our portfolio

I recommend we continue to hold Baxter, especially considering it's stock price increased 130% over the last three years
